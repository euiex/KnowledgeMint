KnowledgeMint

KnowledgeMint is a project exploring decentralized knowledge valuation and token emission through AI-evaluated contributions.

It is based on the idea that new knowledge — such as technical data, 3D models, or documentation — can be tokenized when validated by an AI system. The project is experimental and open to contributors interested in AI, cryptoeconomics, and decentralized knowledge systems.
